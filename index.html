About Us

Why battle Food Wastage?

Food is an important factor in our everyday life. We consume food to generate energy, and we use that energy to bring more bread to the table. Food nurtures our soul and body, and in many countries, it also represents our culture. The process of creating the food that we consume everyday requires a great amount of resources such as water, electricity, and crops, with the coordination between farmers, processors, transportation, and retailers. As complex as it can be, a huge percentage of our food production is going to waste. Therefore, by joining the fight against Food Wastage, we are saving resources for the future world of our children.

How are we battling Food Wastage?

RightOver is a community of suppliers and consumers who are joining the battle against Food Wastage. We create and develop a platform where merchants can easily sell their end-of-the-day food products, while consumers can have it delivered to their doorsteps in perfectly fresh quality and ready for their consumption.

What are the benefits of joining us?

For merchants

We offer a helping hand to prevent discarding end-of-day food products. By joining our platform, not only will you be connected to consumers, your business will also be promoted and recognized as anti food wastage. We also offer to solve the disposal of your tasty products by picking them up at the end of the day and having them delivered to the closest homeless shelters and orphanages on your behalf.

For Delivery Partners

Join us for more work opportunities and save the world from wasting food! Your hard work will be rewarded by standard shipping wage, along with our gratitude for joining in to prevent food wastage!
